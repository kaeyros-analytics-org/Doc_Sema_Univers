**Biomedical Platform**


**User Guide**

.. image:: images/image0EN.png




**Biolab** is an **online biomedical platform** that facilitates the **management of appointments**, **exams**, **patient records**, and **laboratory**, **physician**, and **patient activities**.

**Simple**, **secure**, and **accessible online**, it also **centralizes** **user** and **role management** for **seamless** and **comprehensive tracking**.
