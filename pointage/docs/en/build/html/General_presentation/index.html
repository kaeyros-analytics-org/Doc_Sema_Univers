How the system works

The attendance tracking tool developed by Kaeyros Analytics is an innovative and easy-to-use solution that allows companies to effectively track their employees' arrival and departure times using an already familiar channel: WhatsApp. Designed to minimize the effort involved in managing attendance, the tool is built around an intuitive workflow. Each company site has a QR Code displayed at the entrance. When an employee arrives or leaves the workplace, they simply scan the QR Code with their phone. This scan automatically opens a WhatsApp conversation with a pre-filled message to send. They then click the "Check in" button on the received message to record their arrival or departure time.

ℹ️
Two check-ins per day

Each employee is entitled to two check-ins per day: the first is recorded as their arrival time and the second as their departure time. All check-ins are recorded in real time on the platform and can be viewed daily, weekly, monthly or yearly.

Reliability & anti-fraud

To ensure reliability and prevent any fraud attempts, the system relies on geolocation. When a company is created on the platform, the GPS coordinates of each site are recorded. At every check-in, the employee's geographic position is compared to the site's location. If the employee is more than 100 metres away from the workplace, the system blocks the check-in, preventing any employee from checking in from home or an unauthorized location.

⚠️
Geolocation must be enabled

For the location verification to work correctly, the employee must have geolocation enabled on their phone at the time of check-in.

Absence management

Beyond check-ins, the platform offers comprehensive absence management. When an employee does not show up and performs no check-in, the system automatically detects this and records them as absent. Absences can also be manually entered by a manager, specifying the duration and type of absence — sick leave, maternity leave, bereavement leave, etc. Absence types are fully configurable according to each company's internal policies.

Shifts & work schedules

To structure working time, the platform includes a shift and schedule system. A shift defines a work time slot, such as a morning or night team. A schedule groups one or more shifts, the associated working days, and the employees concerned. Based on these schedules, the system automatically evaluates, for each employee, their late arrivals, overtime and absences, giving managers a clear and reliable view of attendance within their organisation.
